Blackjack Basics

For those that do not know the rules of blackjack (which will be integral to writing this little application!):

  • You take a shuffled deck of cards and deal 2 to each player
  • The card number represents the cards value
  • Picture cards (Jack, Queen and King) are all worth 10
  • Initially, for the purposes of this exercise, aces are worth 1 point (you can change this later if you fancy the challenge)
  • Players can ‘hit’ (add another card to their hand) or ‘stick’ (keep with their current hand)
  • The aim is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over (going bust)
  • You can continue to gain more cards (by Hitting) until you go bust or stick.
  • You will need to have a fake dealer who you play against. (The dealer will play without any user input, making decisions autonomously)
  • Closest to 21 wins
